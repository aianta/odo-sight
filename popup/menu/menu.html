If the odobot server is running, it may be using a self signed certificate that is preventing Odo Bot from connecting!

Click the link below to open a new tab and accept the risks of connecting to a server with a self signed certificate to continue. After you have accepted the certificate you may close the tab.

The extension will retry the connection in 20 seconds and only persist if the issue persists.
